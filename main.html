How To Use

This tutorial is designed to make your navigation of our various sections straightforward.

Equipment

Explore features such as filtered search, addition, browsing equipment names and brands, editing, and accessing detailed information about inventory items.

Customer

Manage customer information with ease. Utilize filtered search, addition, browsing by name, email, and phone, as well as the ability to edit customer details and access comprehensive customer profiles.

Maintenance

Access and manage data tables on the website. Utilize filtered search, explore tables of information, add new tables, and edit fields within these tables.

Reports

Stay informed by visiting the Reports page. Utilize filtered search and have the option to edit these reports or view detailed information about them.

Repairs

Efficiently handle repair orders. Use the filtered search to find specific orders and their owners. Edit and access additional details with a simple click.
